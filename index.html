What is STEMcomp 2019?

STEMcomp 2019 is the first student-run STEM Olympics competition in the WRDSB! It's a one-day competition where groups of 4 compete in challenges and a group project, as well as engage in workshops to deepen their understanding and appreciation for STEM. Run out of Kitchener-Waterloo Collegiate Institute and Vocational School in Kitchener, Ontario.
